No membership needed to get started! We offer a free trial for new members. Click here to get your free trial.

Yes! If you love The Beehive and want more out of your membership, you can upgrade at any time. Simply give us a call at (123)-456-7890 or email us at thebeehive@thebeehive.com

Can I place my membership on pause?

Yes! If you need to put a pause on your membership, simply give us a call at (123)-456-7890 or email us at thebeehive@thebeehive.com.

Can I bring my dog with me?

Woof! We are dog-friendly. We welcome all dogs with proof of vaccination. Please email thebeehive@thebeehive.com with a picture of your pup and their medical records prior to bringing them to The Beehive.
